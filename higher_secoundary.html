The Foundation Offers the Following Services to the Youths

At ORBITWAVE FOUNDATION, we are dedicated to equipping the youth with essential skills and resources to help them achieve their full potential. By providing hands-on training and mentorship, we aim to empower young individuals and create a pathway to success in their personal and professional lives.


The programs and services offered by the foundation are designed to enhance employability, foster independence, and contribute to the economic growth of our community and nation.


1. Installation of Dishes
– Youth are trained to install and maintain satellite dishes such as DStv and Canal Plus. This skill helps them explore job opportunities in the fast-growing telecommunications industry.


2. Installation of Internet Routers and Optic Fibre
– We provide training in setting up internet routers and installing optic fiber systems, preparing the youth to meet the demands of the ever-expanding digital world.


3. Installation of CCTV Cameras
– This program focuses on teaching the youth to install and configure CCTV systems, equipping them with expertise to contribute to safety and security needs.


4. Repairing Electrical Appliances
– Comprehensive training is offered for repairing mobile phones, televisions, decoders, fridges, and other appliances, enabling the youth to start small businesses or find employment in this sector.


5. Career Counseling and Job Placement
– The foundation helps trained youth identify opportunities and connects them with potential employers to secure a better living and long-term stability.


6. Youth Sensitization Programs
– We conduct workshops to help youth understand the value of education and skills acquisition, demonstrating how they can earn a livelihood through honest and skilled work rather than resorting to crime or illegal activities.


7. Customer-Focused Solutions
– Along with empowering the youth, we extend our services to customers by offering high-quality installations, repairs, and security solutions delivered with professionalism and expertise.


At ORBITWAVE FOUNDATION, we believe that by investing in the youth, we are investing in the future. Together, we can create a world where every young individual is empowered to succeed.
